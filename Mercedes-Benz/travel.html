Travel.

The world is diverse, colourful and huge. Those who travel by car make their way to their destination and experience stories on the way that would have remained hidden from an air traveller. This is how a Mercedes-Benz becomes a kind of home on the way. Along the route, special places inspire drivers. There are photos not only at the destination – but also on the road again and again, when the beauty of a coastline or wild animals on the roadside take our breath away. Whether it’s a new model or a classic, a Mercedes-Benz reliably guides its passengers to the remotest places and makes the road trip a wonderful, unforgettable event.
