The Team.

Every type of automobile from Daimler AG is perceived first of all on a sensual level. Barely anyone can resist this emotional impact, because long before any technical data or innovations are revealed, desires are waked up by the design alone. One of the main tasks of Daimler design team is to create desires in the beholder. Depending on the product, various teams from different countries work together to realise dream cars.













The Studios.

The designers at the five Mercedes-Benz Advanced Design studios travel far into the future every day. They often think a decade or more ahead. And they look beyond their local markets and absorb different and new trends from other continents and cultures. The Advanced Design teams are looking to the future with a global perspective, fostering cultural exchange and researching trends. This is why four of the five Mercedes-Benz Advanced Design studios are based abroad.
